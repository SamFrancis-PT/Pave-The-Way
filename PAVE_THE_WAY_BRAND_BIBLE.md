## 8. CLIENT TRANSFORMATIONS

### Damo — Fit Into His 50s
**Image:** /images/Damo.jpg
**Tag:** FIT INTO HIS 50S
**Duration:** 18 months of 1-on-1 training
**Result:** Complete body recomposition — fat loss, muscle gain and a lasting fitness habit.
**Story:** Damo knew he was ready for a change. Tired of feeling weak with a body that didn't reflect how he felt inside, post-covid he had stacked on weight. A busy professional who was active in his younger years, he had lost touch with training and the discipline to prioritise his health. He began hesitantly, wanting to do a few months and see how he'd go. Once he started seeing results the months flew by. He began with 2-3 sessions a week, then completed Sam's couch-to-5km programme — a catalyst for change. Damo dialled in his diet and found a passion for living a healthy lifestyle, now training 5 days a week (3 weights, 2 cardio). Training is now a priority and non-negotiable. He has not looked back.

### Paul — Fit Into His 50s
**Duration:** Started August 2024
**Story:** Paul began training driven by something deeply personal — watching his father age, becoming frail and unable to manage simple everyday tasks, was his wake-up call. A complete beginner, his body was stiff and achey. He committed to two sessions per week, learning compound lifts with correct technique. Despite a slow start he never missed a session, often practising techniques at home. Month after month his body composition changed radically. He cleaned up his nutrition, cutting back on junk food and alcohol. Paul now sets the example for his two kids — showing them that prioritising health is essential. The sessions never get easier, but that has never stopped him. This has become a lifestyle.
